About Us

Transforming shopping into an experience. Choose between online and local stores just by one click and experience shopping with AI.

Shoppinator was built for consumers, by consumers! Ever see something and wanted to buy it, but didn't know where to get it? Shoppinator has made this process easy by uploading a photo and with intelligent AI can detect what the item is, and then can list stores online or nearby all in a simple intuitive layout.

Though e-commerce is growing faster than any other retail sector, the opportunity for brick-and-mortar retailers isn’t extinct; it’s simply a different value proposition in the eyes of the customer
